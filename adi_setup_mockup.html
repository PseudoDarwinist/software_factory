EU261 Regulation Overview
Added by Sarah Chen ‚Ä¢ 2 days ago
EU261 establishes passenger rights for flight disruptions. Key thresholds: 3+ hour delays require compensation, overnight delays require hotel accommodation. Mechanical issues are airline responsibility, weather is extraordinary circumstance.

Added by Mike Rodriguez ‚Ä¢ 1 week ago
